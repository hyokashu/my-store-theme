How long does delivery take?
▼
Standard shipping typically takes 3–7 business days for metro areas, and 5–10 business days for regional areas. Express options may be available at checkout depending on your postcode.
How much does shipping cost?
▼
Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight. Orders over $250 receive free standard shipping automatically.
How can I track my order?
▼
Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package directly on the carrier's website.
What if my order arrives damaged?
▼
If your order arrives damaged, please take photos immediately and contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We'll arrange a replacement or refund as quickly as possible.
Do you offer click & collect?
▼
At this time we are an online-only store and do not offer click & collect. All orders are shipped directly to your provided address.

Still have shipping questions?

What is your return policy?
▼
We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for products that are factory sealed and in original, unopened condition. Due to the nature of trading card products, we cannot accept returns on opened items.
How do I start a return?
▼
To initiate a return, please contact us via our contact page with your order number and reason for return. We'll respond within 1 business day with return instructions. Do not ship items back without prior authorisation.
Who pays for return shipping?
▼
Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item was received damaged or incorrect. We recommend using tracked shipping for your return as we cannot be held responsible for lost parcels.
When will I receive my refund?
▼
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we'll process your refund within 3–5 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Please allow additional time for your bank to process the transaction.
Are sale items returnable?
▼
Items purchased during a sale or promotional event are considered final sale and are not eligible for return, unless the item arrived damaged or was incorrectly sent.
What if I received the wrong item?
▼
We're sorry! Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with your order number and a photo of the item received. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.
