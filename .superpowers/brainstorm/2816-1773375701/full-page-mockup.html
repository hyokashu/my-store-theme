How It Started

We got tired of crushed corners and sketchy reseals.

Two collectors, one too many bad purchases. We decided to build the store we always wished existed — where every product is factory sealed, every shipment is insured, and every customer is treated like a fellow collector.

Supply District launched from Melbourne with one goal: be the most trusted source for sealed TCG product in Australia.
